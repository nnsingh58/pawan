About Us

India Travel Planner is a platform designed to make travel across India easy, affordable, and safe. Whether you're planning a short trip between bus stands or a long journey, we connect you with the best ride options. Our mission is to empower travelers with real-time fare estimates, route planning, and emergency support, ensuring a seamless experience.

How It Works

1. Register with your mobile number linked to Aadhar.
2. Verify your identity with OTP.
3. Plan your travel by entering start and destination bus stands.
4. Get fare estimates and publish your ride!
Our platform also offers emergency assistance at the click of a button.
